### Principles over playbooks

We invest based on first principles, not pattern-matching. We back map-makers, not followers

We look beyond noise. Technical rigor and long-term strategy matter more than chasing the latest buzz

### Founder as compass

The strongest ideas begin with founders who see clearly before others do. We build around that clarity

### Evolution, not prediction

We don’t pretend to forecast the future. We support variation, fast iteration, and adapting to the unknown
