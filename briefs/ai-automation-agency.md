# Niche Design Brief — AI automation agency (done-for-you, SMB-facing)

> Niche-level conventions only. Cached 2026-07-06 from a full Step 1.5 research pass.

- **Niche & read:** done-for-you AI automation / AI receptionist vendors selling to SMB owner-operators. The niche's mode is a light SaaS template: friendly "never miss another call" headline, emoji or mascot hero, industry-tag pill wall, three-column pricing table. Award-tier craft (dark theme + one hot accent, GSAP scrub, site-as-live-demo-of-capability) is unclaimed — a dark, senior, proof-led site instantly outclasses the field.
- **Default tier:** 3–4 (5 for brand-forward revamps).
- **Layout & density:** 12-col; alternate vast type-led hero moments (1 idea per viewport) with dense "spec block" strips (metrics, logos, mono labels, like a datasheet). Converting sequence: hero → proof strip (animated metrics + logos) → live demo (audio of a real call) → how-it-works (3–4 steps) → outcomes/ROI → industries → pricing/book-a-call → single hard CTA.
- **Motion character:** the site itself is the capability demo. ONE pinned scrub set-piece max (hero narrative); step-based reveals for how-it-works; transform/opacity only; mandatory reduced-motion fallback. Overdone in genre: parallax everywhere, horizontal-scroll on text, scroll-jacking past the hero.
- **Native components:** hero = full-bleed set-piece + one blunt promise + one CTA + "hear a real call" audio trigger (the niche's strongest trust move); nav = minimal fixed bar, 3–4 links + one accent "Book a call" CTA; cards = mono-labeled stat cards with real numbers, mechanical (not floaty) hover; trust = animated counters + logo strip high on page, named testimonials with business type, a live demo phone number is the ultimate trust signal.
- **Imagery direction:** real environments over abstractions — shop floors, service trucks at dusk, dispatch boards, hands on tools; dark with sodium-vapor/tungsten practical lighting, long shadows, shallow grain; machinery macro details as texture blocks. Never robots, brains, orbs, or headset stock photos.
- **Image query seeds:** ["machine shop night sodium vapor lighting CNC lathe cinematic dark amber", "service technician truck dusk headlights phone call moody industrial", "macro machined metal parts amber light dark background engineering detail"]
- **One deliberate departure (proven concept):** replace the SaaS pricing table with configured pricing — an interactive build-sheet / job-ticket where the visitor specs their system and gets one number and one CTA. No competitor does this.
- **Anti-references (observed slop in niche):** purple/indigo-to-cyan gradient meshes; glassmorphism card spam; emoji or cartoon-robot hero mascots; glowing brain / circuit-head iconography; waveform-with-sparkles visualizations; unstyled pill-cloud of 20 industry tags; three-column pricing wall with "Most Popular" ribbon; "Revolutionize/Supercharge your business with AI" voice; light-gray-on-white SaaS minimalism.
- **Galleries/references:** Awwwards design-agencies category; wavespace.agency best-examples roundup (Noomo etc.); competitor baselines: goodcall.com, myaifrontdesk.com, aiintegrating.com.
