# Niche-Brief Cache

One cached **Niche Design Brief** per niche, named `briefs/<niche-slug>.md` (lowercase, hyphenated: `restaurant`, `salon`, `contractor`, `dentist`, `gym`, `coffee-shop`, …). The brief format is defined in SKILL.md → Step 1.5; the Mexican-restaurant worked example there is the canonical shape.

**Who reads/writes it:**

- **Demo Mode** — a cache hit replaces Step 1.5 research entirely; a cache miss means research once (time-boxed), then write the brief here so the next lead in that niche is free.
- **Full pipeline** — writes a niche-level copy here after every Step 1.5 research pass, and may read an existing one as a research head start (never as a substitute for the research).

**Rules:**

- Niche-level design conventions **only**. Never write lead/client/business data into a cached brief — this repo is public.
- The `Tier` field records the tier-niche-map default; Demo Mode ignores it (fixed at Tier 3).
- Refresh a brief when it feels stale (galleries drift) — roughly yearly, or whenever a generation from it reads dated.

Seeded with the five highest-volume outreach niches: **restaurant · salon · contractor · dentist · gym**.
