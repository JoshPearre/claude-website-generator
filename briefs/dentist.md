## Niche Design Brief — dentist / dental practice

- Niche & read: patient-acquisition site — reassuring, clean, modern-clinical-but-human; must lower anxiety, not raise it
- Tier: 2 — Restrained Professional (tier-niche-map default; Demo Mode runs it at 3)
- Design direction: human-approachable + accent override
- Palette mood: cool-leaning clean neutrals with one calm accent, soft contrast, light default
  → --accent ≈ oklch(60% 0.11 220) calm sky/teal (never surgical green or alarm red)
- Type character: rounded-friendly sans display, medium weights, high-legibility body
- Imagery direction: bright genuine smiles + calm modern office interiors, soft daylight, diverse patients, real photography
- Image query seeds: ["dentist patient smiling bright modern clinic", "dental office interior clean daylight minimal", "family smiling portrait natural light"]
- Layout & density: airy; hero/services/insurance+financing/team/reviews/book/footer
- Motion character: light reveals only; registry: none
- Shape/texture: soft radii (12–16px), gentle tinted section bands, no shadows-on-shadows
- Native components: hero=smile photo + book CTA + phone · nav=sticky, book button · card=service cards with icon + two lines
- ui-ux-pro-max: style=calm clinical-modern · UX focus=appointment CTA, insurance clarity, accessibility (WCAG AA)
- Prompt categories: hero-sections, navigation, cards-and-grids, forms-and-inputs, footers
- One deliberate departure: one friendly oversized stat ("30 years of gentle care") set in the accent color
- Anti-references: tooth-mascot cartoons, blinding blue-white gradients, drill/chair close-ups (anxiety triggers), too-perfect-teeth stock macros
- Reference brands: Tend, Overjet, one strong local practice · galleries: SiteInspire "Health", Land-book "Medical"
